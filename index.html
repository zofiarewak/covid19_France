COVID-19 Pandemic Dashboard

Interactive epidemiological and vaccination analysis of France and the European Union

1. Infection waves & mortality dynamics

A synchronized timeline comparing daily new cases and deaths per million inhabitants to visualize pandemic waves.

2. Global vaccination overview (Year-end)

Macro-level comparison of cumulative total vaccinations status per 100 people across consecutive years.

3. Detailed vaccination progress (2020-2022)

A close-up monthly look showing the speed, timeline, and stabilization of the vaccine rollout.

4. Policy response vs infection waves

A dual-axis timeline analyzing how government intervention strictness (stringency index) responded to or mitigated actual infection peaks.

5. Predictive modeling & trend analysis

Polynomial regression comparing actual pandemic decline against extrapolated forecasts based on early waves (2020-2022).

6. Animated Pandemic Trajectory

Dynamic Hans Rosling-style simulation mapping total cases against infection acceleration (bubble size indicates daily mortality rate).
